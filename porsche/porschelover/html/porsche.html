In a world quick to celebrate novelty and newness, it is rare to find a design that remains unchanged for the best part of a century. the game-changing Porsche 911, which launched in 1963, has always stayed true to its original vision. “When you think about the Porsche 911, you immediately picture this iconic silhouette. it has unique proportions and a timeless elegance,” says Daniela Milosevic, who heads up design strategy across fashion and style as part of the Porsche design department, explaining how it was the unorthodox decision to position the engine in the rear of the 911 that gave the car its instantly recognisable shape.
